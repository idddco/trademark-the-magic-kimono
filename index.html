Once upon a time, there was a Farmer who lived near Golden Mountain. He and his wife had two children, a girl named Maya and a boy named Karma. The Farmer gre beans, carrots and corn on his 10 acre farm.


The Farmer, as a young man, had gone to Cornell University's College of Agriculture and Life Sciences and had learned all the methods to create genetically modified seed. He bought the farm after a career on Golden Mountain. He had found a wife, Chutney while at University, and they had wanted to raise their children, Maya and Karma, away from Golden Mountain.
